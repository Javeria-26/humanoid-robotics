# Assessment Questions

## Chapter 1: Digital Twins in Robotics

1. Define digital twin in the context of robotics and explain its role in simulation.
2. What are the key benefits of using digital twins in robotics development?
3. How do Gazebo and Unity complement each other in digital twin implementation?

## Chapter 2: Physics Simulation (Gazebo)

1. Explain the key physics simulation capabilities of Gazebo.
2. Describe the process of environment modeling in Gazebo.
3. How does collision detection work in Gazebo and why is it important?

## Chapter 3: High-Fidelity Environments (Unity)

1. What are Unity's key visualization and rendering capabilities for robotics?
2. Describe how Unity supports human-robot interaction scenarios.
3. What are the primary use cases for Unity in robotics simulation?

## Chapter 4: Sensor Simulation

1. Compare the simulation characteristics and limitations of LiDAR, depth cameras, and IMUs.
2. Explain how environmental factors affect sensor performance in simulation.
3. Describe the importance of realistic sensor data characteristics in robotics simulation.
