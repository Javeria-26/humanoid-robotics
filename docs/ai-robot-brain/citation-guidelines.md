# Citation Guidelines for AI-Robot Brain Research Module

## APA Format Standards

All references in this module must follow the American Psychological Association (APA) 7th edition format. This ensures academic rigor and consistency across all chapters.

## Common Reference Types

### Journal Articles
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

### Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher Name.

### Book Chapters
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In A. Editor & B. Editor (Eds.), *Title of book* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher Name.

### Conference Papers
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year, Month). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of the Conference Name* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

### Technical Documentation
NVIDIA Corporation. (Year). *Isaac Sim documentation*. NVIDIA Developer. URL

### Websites
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Date). Title of webpage. *Website Name*. URL

## Quality Standards

- At least 50% of references must be peer-reviewed sources
- All sources must be verifiable and current (preferably within 5-7 years for technical content)
- Each chapter must include at least 3 references
- The module must contain at least 15 references total
- All citations must include DOI or URL for verification when available

## In-Text Citation Format

Use author-date format for in-text citations:
- Single author: (Author, Year)
- Multiple authors: (Author, Author, & Author, Year) for first citation; (Author et al., Year) for subsequent citations
- Direct quotes: (Author, Year, p. #)
