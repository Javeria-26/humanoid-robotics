# Quickstart: Digital Twin Simulation Module

## Overview
This quickstart guide helps you understand and implement the digital twin simulation module focusing on Gazebo and Unity for robotics education.

## Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of robotics concepts
- Familiarity with simulation environments
- Academic research mindset

## Getting Started

### 1. Understanding Digital Twins
- Define digital twin concepts in robotics
- Identify the role of digital twins in simulation
- Explore the benefits of digital twin technology

### 2. Physics Simulation with Gazebo
- Learn about environment modeling
- Understand gravity and force simulation
- Explore collision detection mechanisms

### 3. High-Fidelity Simulation with Unity
- Create realistic 3D environments
- Implement human-robot interaction scenarios
- Understand visualization capabilities

### 4. Sensor Simulation
- Study LiDAR simulation techniques
- Understand depth camera modeling
- Learn about IMU simulation and limitations
